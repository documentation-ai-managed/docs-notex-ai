## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">

- An active account on your preferred meeting platform (Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or Google Meet)
- A team email for invitations
- Browser access to `https://dashboard.notex.ai` (NoteX AI dashboard)

</Callout>

NoteX AI supports hybrid, online, and in-person meetings across 50+ languages.

## Create and Verify Your Account

Follow these steps to set up your NoteX AI account quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://dashboard.notex.ai/signup` and enter your email and team name.

    Create a strong password and complete the CAPTCHA.

    <Image
      src="https://example.com/signup-screenshot.png"
      alt="NoteX AI signup form with email and password fields"
      width="800"
      height="500"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification email from NoteX AI.

    Click the verification link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Meeting Platform" icon="link">
    In the dashboard, select your platform (Zoom, Teams, or Meet) and authorize NoteX AI.

    Grant microphone and screen share permissions for capture.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your account is now ready for team collaboration.

## Invite Team Members and Set Permissions

Add your global team and control access.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate Invite Link" icon="share-2">
    Navigate to **Team** > **Invites** in the dashboard.

    Click **Create Invite** and set expiration (e.g., 7 days).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share and Manage Permissions" icon="users">
    Copy the invite link and share via email or Slack.

    Assign roles: **Admin** (full access), **Viewer** (read-only transcripts), or **Editor** (summaries and actions).

    | Role     | Transcription | Summary Editing | Action Items |
    |----------|---------------|-----------------|--------------|
    | Admin    | Full         | Yes             | Yes          |
    | Editor   | Full         | Yes             | View         |
    | Viewer   | Read-only    | No              | View         |
  </Step>
</Steps>

Team members join instantly upon clicking the link.

## Join Your First Meeting

Enable NoteX AI capture across platforms.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Zoom" icon="video">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Start Meeting">
        Open Zoom and start or join a meeting.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add NoteX AI">
        Click **More** > **Invite NoteX AI Bot** or paste `notex-ai-bot@notex.ai`.

        Approve bot entry and grant permissions.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Microsoft Teams" icon="microsoft-teams">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Add Bot">
        In Teams, go to **People** > **Add NoteX AI Bot**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enable Capture">
        Toggle **NoteX AI Transcription** in meeting chat.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google Meet" icon="google">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Invite Bot">
        Click **Activities** > **NoteX AI** or dial `+1-555-NOTEXAI`.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Start Capture">
        Confirm language detection and start.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

NoteX AI auto-detects languages and begins transcription.

## Review Your Transcript and Summary

Access results immediately after the meeting ends.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Transcript" icon="file-text">
    Go to **Meetings** in the dashboard.

    Click your meeting to see full multilingual transcript with timestamps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Summary and Actions" icon="list">
    Scroll to **AI Summary** for key points, decisions, and action items.

    Edit and assign tasks directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share" icon="share">
    Export as PDF or share secure link with non-users.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="success">
  Congratulations! You've completed your first NoteX AI meeting. Transcripts are available in seconds.
</Callout>
