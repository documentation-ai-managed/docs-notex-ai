### **Step 1: Choose Your Sign-In Method**

On the **Log in to NoteX AI** home screen, you have four options:

- **Continue with Google/Apple/Microsoft:** Fast-track your login using your existing professional or personal accounts.

- **Continue with Email:** Sign in using a dedicated email account registered directly with NoteX.

### **Step 2: Enter Your Credentials (Email Option)**

If you choose to sign in via email, you will be directed to the **Log in to your account** screen:

- **Email:** Enter your work email address (e.g., <a href="mailto:thuy.la@sotatek.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">[thuy.la@sotatek.com](mailto:thuy.la@sotatek.com)</a>).

- **Password:** Enter your secure password.

- **Login:** Click the black button to access your dashboard.

### **Step 3: Passwordless Login (Optional)**

To avoid the hassle of managing multiple passwords, you can select **Login without Password**. The system will send a secure verification code or a direct magic link to your inbox.

## **FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions**

1\. What should I do if I forgot my password?

On the login screen, click Forgot Password?. You will receive an email with instructions to reset your password within 30 seconds.

2\. Can I switch between Google and Microsoft accounts?

Yes. However, please note that different sign-in methods may create separate accounts unless they are linked to the same primary email address.

3\. Why haven't I received my Passwordless Login email?

Please check your Spam or Promotions folders. If it still hasn't arrived after one minute, click "Resend."
