---
title: No Transcript Generated After Meeting
description: How to resolve missing transcript issues.
---

## Symptoms

- No transcript or summary email after meeting
- "Transcript unavailable" message in dashboard

## Causes
- Bot was not added correctly
- Audio access was restricted

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check bot invitation">
    Ensure `bot@notex.ai` was on the meeting invite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review meeting settings">
    Make sure the meeting allowed recording and audio access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Retry">
    If issue persists, re-invite the bot and check your email spam folder.
  </Step>
</Steps>
