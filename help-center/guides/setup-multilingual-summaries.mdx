---
title: Set Up Multilingual Summaries
description: How to enable summaries in multiple languages.
---

## Overview

NoteX AI can deliver meeting summaries in multiple languages automatically.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Login at `https://dashboard.notex.ai`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Click on "Preferences" > "Summary Languages".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose languages">
    Select one or more languages (e.g. English, Spanish, French).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    All future meeting summaries will be sent in your selected languages.
  </Step>
</Steps>
