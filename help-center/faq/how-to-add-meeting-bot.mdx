description: Steps to invite NoteX AI to your meetings.
---

## Quick Steps

Invite NoteX AI as a participant by adding `bot@notex.ai` to your meeting invite.

## Supported Platforms

- Zoom
- Microsoft Teams
- Google Meet
- In-person (via mobile app)

## What happens next?

The bot joins automatically, records, transcribes, and summarizes the meeting. The summary and transcript are sent by email after the call.
