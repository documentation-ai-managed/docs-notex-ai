## **1. What is this feature for?**

- **Sales & Business Professionals:** Summarize YouTube webinars, industry podcasts, or client call recordings.

- **Operations Managers:** Digitize SOPs and operational docs (PDF/Docx), generate meeting minutes from audio files, and process bulk raw text to extract key insights instantly.

---

## **2. Step-by-Step Guide**

To get started, click the **Import** button on the main dashboard and select your input type:

### **Step 1: Choose your source**

- **Web Links:** Paste links from YouTube, Apple Podcasts, TikTok, or news articles.

- **Upload Files:** Upload documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT, etc.) or audio/video files (MP3, WAV, M4A, MP4, etc.).

  <Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
    *Pro Tip: Toggle&#x20;******Enable multi-file audio uploads******&#x20;to process multiple audio files simultaneously.*
  </Callout>

- **Input Text:** Paste raw text or handwritten notes directly into the text box (up to 300,000 characters).

### **Step 2: Configure settings**

- **Folder:** Select a destination folder (defaults to **All Notes**).

- **Summary Language:** Choose the language for your AI-generated notes (e.g., English or Vietnamese).

- **Additional Instructions (Optional):** Enter specific prompts for the AI (e.g., "Use bullet points," "Extract key timestamps").

### **Step 3: Wrap up**

Click the **AI Summary** button to let the system process your data and generate your notes.

---

## **3. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**

### What file formats are supported?

NoteX supports most standard formats: MP3, WAV, M4A, OGG, MP4, MPEG4, PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT, JSON, and MD.

### What are the upload limits?

The maximum file size per document is 20MB. For audio, the total duration limit is 6 hours.

### Can I summarize content in different languages?

Yes. You can select your preferred output language in the Summary Language section, regardless of the source material's original language.
