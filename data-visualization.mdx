## How to use

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Mindmap">
    <Callout kind="success" collapsed="false">
      Purpose: Visually organize workflows, project structures, or key takeaways from meetings for better clarity.
    </Callout>

    How to use:

    - Navigate to the **Mindmap** tab.

    - Click Create Mindmap. The AI will automatically analyze your text and generate a visual tree diagram.

    - Use Case: A Sales Manager categorizing customer feedback; an Operations Manager mapping out a new workflow.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Slideshow">
    <Callout kind="success" collapsed="false">
      Purpose: Instantly generate professional presentations without the hassle of manual design.
    </Callout>

    How to use:

    - Select the **Slideshow** tab.

    - Under **Slide Count**, choose your desired length (Auto or a range of 4–12 slides).

    - In the preview panel on the right, use the arrows to browse and select a **Theme** (e.g., *Neon Orbit*).

    - Click Create Slideshow.

    - Use Case: Putting together a quick weekly sales report or summarizing training materials for your team.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Quizzes">
    <Callout kind="success" collapsed="false">
      Purpose: Assess your own or your team's understanding of a topic after a project or training session.
    </Callout>

    How to use:

    - Select the **Quizzes** tab.

    - **Quiz Count:** Choose the number of questions.

    - **Quiz Difficulty:** Select your level (Easy, Medium, Hard, or Mixed).

    - **Topic (Optional):** Enter a specific focus area (e.g., "Focus only on discount policies").

    - Click Create Quizzes.

    - Use Case: Testing the sales team's knowledge of a new product launch.Purpose: Store and review key terms, definitions, or technical specifications.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Flashcards">
    <Callout kind="success" collapsed="false">
      Purpose: Store and review key terms, definitions, or technical specifications.
    </Callout>

    How to use:

    - Select the **Flashcards** tab.

    - Adjust the **Card Count** and **Difficulty** settings as needed.

    - Click Create Flashcards to generate a double-sided deck (Question & Answer).

    - Use Case: Mastering industry jargon or new warehouse operating procedures.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Podcast">
    <Callout kind="success" collapsed="false">
      Purpose: Convert your text into an audio conversation - perfect for catching up while driving or multitasking.
    </Callout>

    How to use:

    - Select the **Podcast** tab.

    - **Duration:** Choose your length (30s, 90s, 180s, or 300s).

    - **Dual Speakers:** Toggle this on to create a more engaging dialogue between two people.

    - **Voice:** Choose a voice profile (e.g., Kore, Puck) and click the **Play** button to preview.

    - Click Create Podcast.

    - Use Case: Listening to market updates or internal company announcements in a radio-style format.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

  ## **FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions**
</Tab>

### **What if my notes are too long?**&#x20;

The AI will automatically distill the most important points for your Mindmap or Slideshow. To focus on a specific section, use the **Topic (Optional)** feature to guide the AI.

### **Can I export this data?**&#x20;

Yes, simply use the **Export / Download** feature.

### **What’s unique about the "Shorts" feature?**&#x20;

It allows you to transform your notes into short-form videos featuring popular gaming backgrounds (like *GTA* or *Subway Surfers*) to make sharing on social media more engaging and entertaining.
